Issues with Myst & Fyre

Hello & Welcome To the Blog!

 

It has recently come to my attention that W7s Myst/Fyre has been sending out emails to groups that aren't related to W7s in any way. 

I want to apologize to the people who got these emails from Myst/Fyre.

Myst will be down over the weekend for repairs and general work.

Users who had issues with Myst please email me and I will give a code for a $20 W7s Voucher.

The issue stems from a lack of limits in the Ai that controls parts of Myst/Fyre, and there is other part of code that had issues.

There was safeguards in place to prevent this from happening however those failed.

Myst has been deactivated as of 5:45 PM / Thursday.

W7s will be putting safeguards in place to prevent this from happening again. Also, all Ai models will be tested more to confirm they are good for the task.

As for why the Ai was working then stopped that has to be because the Ai is supposed to learn off itself so it can continue to improve however, as seen earlier, this didn't work.

The other issue is that Myst/Fyre had access to do basically whatever it wanted.

I did notice 5% of W7s go missing but I couldn't find why until 5: 30 PM
